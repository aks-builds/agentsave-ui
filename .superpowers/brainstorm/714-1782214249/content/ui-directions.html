AgentSave — UI Design Directions

Three visual directions for the ultra dashboard. Current UI is flat dark-gray cards with no charts or animation.

Direction A
Midnight Glow
Deep space dark + glassmorphism cards + electric violet/indigo gradients + area charts. Inspired by Linear, Vercel, and Raycast. Soothing and premium-feeling — every card glows subtly. Best for developers who love their tools beautiful.

Direction B
Data Terminal
Matrix-green on deep black, monospace fonts mixed with modern sans, real-time sparkline bars, framework leaderboard table with live-update feel. Inspired by Grafana, Datadog, Warp terminal. Very developer-native — feels like you're looking at the matrix. Focused and minimal.
